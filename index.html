About

A family firm, built to go anywhere.

Pansolve began the way it operates: with two people who take problems personally. Founded by Anu and Vikram, we started our working lives in the New York–New Jersey corridor and fell for California along the way — and we've carried both coasts' habits with us: East Coast rigor, West Coast optimism.
