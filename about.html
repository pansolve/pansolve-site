Expert-led services, built to scale with AI.

People-first. AI-enhanced. Accuracy always.

Everyone’s been sold the same story: automate it, and the hard work disappears. In reality? Claims get denied. Books don’t reconcile. Models sound confident and get it wrong.

Pansolve takes a deliberate approach - expert human judgment first, with AI introduced selectively and always subject to expert review, applied only where it adds genuine value.

We’re a global expert-services firm working across payer and provider operations, accounting and finance, and AI training and data services. Every engagement is handled by trained specialists who verify, correct, and refine the work at every stage. We’re building AI-enabled capabilities to make that expert-led model faster and more scalable, but never to replace the judgment at its core.

Why “Pansolve”?

Pan is Greek for “all” - it reflects our belief that complex operational challenges are best solved with qualified people at the center, not automated out of the process. It’s also our direction as a firm: built to grow across practices, not stay confined to one.

AI produces a fast first draft. Our people make it final.

- Pansolve Leadership
