01 / Procure-to-Pay (P2P)

Take control of spend, strengthen supplier relationships, and eliminate payment leakage.

What we manage

  • Purchase requisition and PO management
  • Vendor onboarding and supplier master data upkeep
  • Invoice receipt, three-way matching, and exception handling
  • Payment processing, disbursement, and bank reconciliation support
  • Duplicate-payment prevention, policy enforcement, and tax/regulatory compliance
  • Spend analytics and P2P performance dashboards
Outcomes clients see

Faster PO turnaround

Higher invoice touchless-processing rates

Fewer payment errors

Stronger audit readiness

02 / Order-to-Cash (O2C)

Accelerate cash conversion and give your revenue cycle the discipline it needs.

What we manage

  • Order entry, credit review, and billing accuracy
  • Invoice generation and dispatch
  • Collections management and customer dispute resolution
  • Cash application and reconciliation
  • Credit risk monitoring and bad-debt reporting
  • Revenue and receivables analytics
Outcomes clients see

Shorter days sales outstanding (DSO)

Reduced unapplied cash

Cleaner receivables aging

03 / Record-to-Report (R2R)

Close faster, report with confidence, and keep the books audit-ready every period.

What we manage

  • General ledger accounting and journal entry processing
  • Month-end and year-end close management
  • Account reconciliations and balance sheet substantiation
  • Fixed asset and intercompany accounting
  • Financial statement preparation and management reporting
  • Variance analysis and close-cycle analytics
Outcomes clients see

Shorter close cycles

Fewer reconciling items

Management reports your leadership team can trust on day one of the new period
