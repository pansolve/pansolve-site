Cash out, carefully. Cash in, faster.

03 / Reconciliation & Controls

Every account, tied out. Every difference, explained.

What it is

Regular reconciliation of bank, credit card, payroll, and balance-sheet accounts — with every variance investigated and documented, not carried forward.

Why it matters

Unreconciled differences are where errors and fraud hide. "We'll figure it out at year-end" is how small discrepancies become expensive audits.

What our team does

  • Reconciles bank and credit card accounts weekly or monthly
  • Investigates every variance to the transaction level — no plugged numbers
  • Maintains balance-sheet schedules that match the general ledger
  • Builds simple internal controls sized for a growing business
